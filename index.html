Stein's Fishing Tale

Stein’s Fishing Tale is a survival, role-playing, fishing game made in Unity with four partners where the player will act as a scientist who recently moved to an island. To survive on that island, the player will need to continue fishing as it’s the only reasonable source of food while being very careful around them as some of the fishes have become very aggressive due to nuclear radiation. Throughout this process of exploration and survival, the player will eventually learn the history of the world and discover the protagonist’s true identity.
